FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our membership programs, eligibility, and benefits.

How do I apply for membership?

You can apply for membership by visiting our contact page and filling out the membership inquiry form. Our team will review your application and get back to you with the appropriate membership category and plan options based on your profile and requirements.

Can I upgrade my membership plan?

Yes, you can upgrade your membership plan at any time. Simply contact our membership team, and they will help you transition to a longer-duration plan with prorated pricing based on your remaining membership period.

What laboratory facilities are available?

Members have access to our ISO 9001:2015 certified laboratory facilities through our collaboration with GANESH Scientific Research Foundation. This includes molecular biology, microbiology, environmental testing, and computational analysis infrastructure.

Are the certifications recognized?

Yes, all certifications issued by SARG Research Biolabs are backed by our registration with the Ministry of MSME, Government of India, and our collaboration with ISO 9001:2015 certified GANESH Scientific Research Foundation. These certifications validate your hands-on laboratory competence and research skill development.

Can institutions apply for group memberships?

Absolutely! We offer Institutional Membership specifically designed for colleges, universities, research laboratories, and training institutes. This includes benefits like joint research projects, faculty development, student training programs, and shared infrastructure access.

Is online participation available?

Yes, most of our training programs follow a hybrid model with weekday online theory sessions and weekend laboratory training. This allows participants from different locations to benefit from our programs while ensuring essential hands-on practical experience.
