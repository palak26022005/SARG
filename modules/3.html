Food Biotechnology Training

Immerse yourself in the exciting field of food biotechnology with our comprehensive training program. Gain hands-on experience in enhancing food quality, safety, and sustainability through biotechnological techniques and advanced applications.

30 Days

Intensive Training

Food Innovation & Safety

Transform the Future of Food Production

Gain hands-on experience in the application of biotechnological techniques to enhance the quality, safety, and sustainability of food products, including genetic modification, fermentation, and enzymatic processes.

Explore the latest advancements in food biotechnology, including bioprocessing, bioinformatics, and nanotechnology, and discover their potential to revolutionize food production and address global challenges.

Learn from industry experts who will guide you through practical laboratory exercises, covering topics such as food preservation, functional foods, and novel ingredient development, equipping you with valuable skills to navigate the complex landscape of the food industry and contribute to its sustainable growth.

Core Focus Areas

Comprehensive Training Modules

Our program covers essential aspects of food biotechnology from microbiology to advanced molecular analysis.

Food Microbiology

Isolation, enumeration, and identification of microorganisms in food samples

Quality Control

Assessment of food quality including jam, jelly, milk products, and beverages

Biochemical Analysis

Estimation of carbohydrates, proteins, fats, and other nutritional components

Pathogen Detection

Enumeration and isolation of E. coli, Salmonella, Shigella, and Vibrio

Adulteration Testing

Detection of adulterants in various commercial food items and products

Food Preservation

Studies on preservation methods using temperature, radiation, and bacteriocins

Water Analysis

BOD analysis and coliform enumeration in water samples

Molecular Methods

DNA extraction and gel electrophoresis for food analysis

Learning Outcomes

Skills You Will Master

Upon completion, you will possess comprehensive skills essential for food biotechnology and quality control careers.

01

Microbiological Proficiency

Master techniques for isolating, enumerating, and identifying microorganisms in food samples, including pathogens and spoilage organisms.

02

Quality Control Expertise

Conduct comprehensive quality assessments of various food products including dairy, beverages, and processed foods.

03

Analytical Testing

Perform biochemical analysis to determine nutritional content including carbohydrates, proteins, fats, and moisture.

04

Food Safety Assessment

Detect and quantify pathogenic microorganisms and assess food safety parameters according to standards.

05

Adulteration Detection

Identify and test for common adulterants in food products using standard analytical methods.

06

Molecular Techniques

Apply DNA-based methods for microbial identification and food authenticity verification.
