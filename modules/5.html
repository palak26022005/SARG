Master the art of crime scene investigation and forensic analysis. Develop critical thinking and problem-solving abilities to solve complex criminal cases through scientific evidence examination.

Crime Scene Investigation

Become a Forensic Science Expert

Our comprehensive forensic science training provides participants with a hands-on learning experience, immersing them in the practical techniques and methodologies used in crime scene investigation and analysis.

Led by experienced forensic professionals, our training program offers expert guidance, equipping participants with the necessary skills in evidence collection, preservation, and analysis, ensuring accurate and reliable forensic examination.

Through case studies and simulated scenarios, participants develop critical thinking and problem-solving abilities, learning to apply forensic science principles to solve complex criminal cases.

Learning Outcomes

Skills You Will Master

Upon completion, you will possess critical forensic science skills essential for criminal investigation and evidence analysis.

01

Evidence Collection & Preservation

Master proper techniques for collecting, preserving, and documenting physical evidence from crime scenes while maintaining chain of custody.

02

Biological Evidence Analysis

Identify and analyze blood, semen, saliva, and other biological fluids using serological and immunological techniques.

03

Hair & Fiber Examination

Conduct microscopic examination of hair samples to determine human vs animal origin and individual characteristics.

04

DNA Profiling

Perform DNA extraction, quantification, PCR amplification, and interpretation of DNA fingerprinting results.

05

Species Identification

Determine species of origin from biological samples using precipitin tests, immunodiffusion, and molecular methods.

06

Scientific Documentation

Prepare accurate forensic reports, maintain laboratory records, and present scientific evidence for legal proceedings.
