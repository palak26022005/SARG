Research-Based Training

Complete Your Research Project With Expert Guidance

Helix BioGenesis has developed comprehensive 1 month to 6 months offline Project Training, Dissertation, and Summer/Winter Training Programs for students of any stream of Biotechnology and life sciences.

The training programs are designed in collaboration with industry experts to ensure alignment with current trends and demands of the biotechnology sector. The curriculum incorporates the latest advancements, emerging technologies, and relevant industrial practices, giving students a competitive edge in the job market.

Projects include laboratory techniques, experimental methodologies in molecular biology, genetic engineering, cell culture, bioinformatics, and other specialized areas within biotechnology. Certificate will be provided to candidates on completion of the training/project work.

Research Areas

Specialized Research Domains

Choose from our diverse research areas covering cutting-edge biotechnology fields.

Molecular Biology

Gene expression, cloning, PCR techniques, and genetic analysis projects.

Genetic Engineering

Recombinant DNA technology, gene editing, and transgenic studies.

Cell Culture

Animal cell culture, cancer cell lines, and tissue engineering projects.

Bioinformatics

Computational biology, sequence analysis, and database mining.

Microbiology

Microbial diversity, antibiotic resistance, and industrial applications.

Immunology

Immunoassays, antibody production, and vaccine development research.

Plant Biotechnology

Plant tissue culture, genetic modification, and stress resistance studies.

Environmental Biotech

Bioremediation, waste management, and sustainable technology projects.

Pharmaceutical Biotech

Drug discovery, formulation, and therapeutic protein production.

Food Biotechnology

Food processing, preservation, and nutritional enhancement research.

Biochemistry

Enzyme kinetics, protein purification, and metabolic studies.

Forensic Biotech

DNA fingerprinting, forensic analysis, and molecular diagnostics.
