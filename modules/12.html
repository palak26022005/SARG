Live & Interactive Online Industrial Training

SARG Research Biolabs is offering 'Advance Live and Interactive Online Industrial Training' with an online certificate in various streams of biotechnology so that the participants can have an insight into experimental Biotechnology.

"We are offering these programs so that students can have live demonstration-based learning and can get an opportunity to interact with the industry experts."

Our online courses are designed to provide the same quality of education and hands-on learning experience as our offline programs. Techniques in any program will remain the same as in case of Offline Program, ensuring you receive comprehensive training regardless of your location.

FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our online training programs.

How do I access the live sessions?

After successful registration, you will receive the meeting link and password via email and WhatsApp 24 hours before the course begins. Sessions are conducted through Zoom or Google Meet.

Are the techniques covered same as offline programs?

Yes, techniques in any online program remain the same as in case of offline programs. You will receive comprehensive training with live demonstrations of all techniques.

How will I receive my certificate?

Upon successful completion, you will receive a free online certificate immediately. A hard copy of the certificate will be sent to your registered address via Speed Post.

Can I use this certificate for college/university requirements?

Yes, our certification can be used to fulfill college/university requirements for Industrial Training programs. The certificate is recognized by academic institutions.

What if I face technical issues during the session?

Our support team is available to assist you with any technical issues. You can reach out via WhatsApp or email, and we'll help resolve the problem promptly.
