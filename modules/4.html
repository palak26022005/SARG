Core Specializations

Key Training Areas

Comprehensive training across medical biotechnology's critical disciplines and applications.

Clinical Serology

Blood group testing, VDRL tests, serological diagnosis of infectious diseases, and antigen-antibody reactions.

Immunological Assays

ELISA, radial immunodiffusion, Ouchterlony double diffusion, and immunoglobulin analysis techniques.

Biochemical Analysis

Liver function tests, kidney function tests, lipid profiles, and metabolic disease diagnosis.

Medical Microbiology

Clinical sample processing, bacterial identification, antibiotic sensitivity, and pathogen detection.

Molecular Diagnostics

DNA extraction, PCR amplification, gel electrophoresis, and genetic disease diagnosis.

Clinical Hematology

Blood microscopy, hemoglobin determination, parasitic infection diagnosis, and blood cell analysis.

Learning Outcomes

Skills You Will Master

Upon completion, you will possess critical skills essential for medical biotechnology and clinical diagnostics.

01

Clinical Sample Analysis

Master techniques for processing and analyzing blood, urine, and other clinical samples for diagnostic purposes.

02

Immunological Testing

Perform serological tests, ELISA, and other immunoassays for disease diagnosis and immune function assessment.

03

Biochemical Assessment

Conduct comprehensive metabolic testing including liver, kidney, and lipid profiles for clinical evaluation.

04

Microbial Diagnostics

Isolate, identify, and perform antimicrobial susceptibility testing on clinical pathogens.

05

Molecular Analysis

Apply PCR and DNA analysis techniques for genetic disease detection and molecular diagnosis.

06

Quality & Safety

Maintain clinical laboratory standards, safety protocols, and quality assurance in diagnostic procedures.
