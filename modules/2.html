Core Techniques

Master Essential Molecular Techniques

Gain hands-on expertise in fundamental and advanced molecular biology techniques used in research laboratories worldwide.

DNA Extraction

Isolate genomic and plasmid DNA from various sources using standard protocols

PCR Amplification

Amplify specific DNA sequences using polymerase chain reaction techniques

Gel Electrophoresis

Separate and analyze DNA/RNA fragments using agarose gel systems

Restriction Digestion

Cut DNA at specific sequences using restriction endonucleases

Gene Cloning

Insert foreign DNA into vectors and transform into host cells

Blotting Methods

Transfer nucleic acids to membranes for detection and analysis

Quantification

Measure nucleic acid concentration using spectrophotometric methods

Cell Transformation

Introduce recombinant DNA into competent bacterial cells

Learning Outcomes

Skills You Will Develop

Upon completion, you will have mastered critical molecular biology skills essential for modern research and diagnostics.

01

Nucleic Acid Isolation

Expert techniques for extracting and purifying DNA and RNA from bacterial, plant, and other sources with high quality and yield.

02

PCR Mastery

Design primers, optimize conditions, and perform various PCR techniques including colony PCR for molecular screening.

03

Analytical Skills

Quantify and assess purity of nucleic acids using spectrophotometry, fluorometry, and electrophoresis methods.

04

Cloning Expertise

Perform complete cloning procedures from DNA digestion to transformation and screening of recombinant clones.

05

Blotting Techniques

Execute Southern and Northern blotting for DNA and RNA analysis in research applications.

06

Laboratory Competence

Demonstrate proficiency in aseptic techniques, buffer preparation, and maintaining proper laboratory practices.
