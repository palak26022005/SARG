Integrated Training Program

Master Three Core Disciplines in One Program

Our mix module training program offers comprehensive instruction in the latest advancements and techniques in the field, equipping participants with the knowledge and practical skills needed to excel in the biotech industry.

Led by industry experts, our training combines theoretical knowledge with hands-on laboratory experiences, providing a well-rounded understanding of biotechnological principles and applications across microbiology, molecular biology, and biochemistry.

Participants will gain valuable insights into genetic engineering, bioprocessing, and the development of innovative biotech products, preparing them for rewarding careers in healthcare, agriculture, and biotechnology research.

Learning Outcomes

Skills You Will Master

Upon completion, you will possess comprehensive skills across microbiology, molecular biology, and biochemistry.

01

Microbial Culture Techniques

Master aseptic techniques, media preparation, isolation, and identification of microorganisms from various sources.

02

DNA Analysis & Manipulation

Perform DNA extraction, PCR, restriction digestion, gel electrophoresis, and blotting techniques proficiently.

03

Protein Characterization

Execute protein isolation, quantification using Bradford/Lowry methods, and SDS-PAGE analysis.

04

Enzymatic & Immunological Assays

Conduct enzyme activity assays and ELISA techniques for biochemical and immunological analysis.

05

Advanced Instrumentation

Operate Nanodrop/Qubit, PCR machines, electrophoresis systems, and spectrophotometers confidently.

06

Integrated Problem Solving

Apply knowledge from multiple disciplines to design experiments and solve complex biotechnology challenges.
