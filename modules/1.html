Industrial Microbiology Training

Experience the fascinating world of microbiology through our immersive hands-on training program. Engage in practical laboratory exercises and cultivate valuable skills for your scientific journey.

Certified

Training Program

Hands-On Training

Comprehensive Industrial Microbiology Program

Our Industrial Microbiology training program offers an immersive, hands-on learning experience designed to equip you with essential laboratory skills and practical knowledge in microbiology research.

Work closely with experienced instructors who will guide you through experiments, fostering a deep understanding of microbial identification, culture techniques, and safety protocols.

Immerse yourself in a dynamic learning environment that encourages critical thinking, problem-solving, and teamwork, equipping you with the confidence and proficiency to excel in the field of microbiology.

Program Options

Choose Your Training Duration

Select from our comprehensive 15-day or intensive 30-day programs, both designed to provide thorough training in industrial microbiology techniques and applications.

15 Days Program

2 Weeks Intensive Training

Introduction to Good Laboratory Practices and General Safety Instructions
Instrumentation: Autoclave, Laminar Air Flow, Incubator/Shaker, Hot Air Oven, Microscope
Various Media (Solid/Liquid) Preparation and Sterilization
Isolation and Screening of Microorganisms
Serial Dilution Method
Pure Culture Techniques (Streaking, Pouring & Plating)
Culturing & Sub Culturing Methods of Microbes
Isolation and Culturing from Soil and Water Samples
Staining Techniques: Gram Staining, Endospore Staining
IMViC Test: Indole, Methyl Red, Voges-Proskauer, Citrate Utilization
Bacterial Growth Curve Analysis/Growth Kinetics
Antibiotics Sensitivity Test
Culturing Techniques for E. coli or Industrially Important Microorganisms
Isolation and Identification of Enzyme Producing Microbe
Isolation of Antibiotic Producing Microbe by Crowded Plate Method
Isolation of Antibiotic Resistance (MDR) Microbe from Soil Sample

30 Days Program

4 Weeks Comprehensive Training

All 15-day program modules included
Isolation and Culturing of Microbes from Air Samples
Components, Use and Care of Compound Microscope
Advanced Staining: Capsular Staining, Flagellar Staining
Enzymatic Tests: Catalase Activity, Oxidase Activity
Optimization of Growth Conditions for Industrial Microorganisms
Fungal Staining & Actinomycetes Staining
Chemical Control of Microbes
Effect of Temperature on Growth of Bacteria
Effect of pH on Growth of Bacteria
Isolation of Lipolytic Microbes from Butter
Effect of pH, Temperature, Pressure, Inhibitors on Growth
Effect of Physical & Chemical Mutagens on Growth of Microbes

Learning Outcomes

What You Will Achieve

Upon completion of this program, you will have developed comprehensive skills in industrial microbiology and laboratory techniques.

01

Laboratory Proficiency

Master essential laboratory techniques including media preparation, sterilization, and aseptic handling procedures.

02

Microbial Isolation

Gain expertise in isolating and culturing microorganisms from various environmental samples.

03

Identification Techniques

Learn multiple staining methods and biochemical tests for accurate microbial identification.

04

Instrumentation Skills

Become proficient in operating and maintaining essential microbiology laboratory equipment.

05

Research Methodology

Understand research design, experimental procedures, and data analysis in microbiology.

06

Safety Compliance

Implement proper safety protocols and maintain ethical standards in laboratory work.
