Who We Are

A Research Organization Dedicated to Scientific Excellence

SARG Research Biolabs is a non-government, autonomous research organization registered with the Ministry of MSME, Government of India, and established in the National Capital Territory of Delhi.

Founded in 2024, it functions as a research division of the Institute for Applied Research and Training (IART), in collaboration with GANESH Scientific Research Foundation (ISO 9001:2015 Certified Research and Testing Laboratory).

We are dedicated to advancing excellence in Life Sciences, Biotechnology, Environmental Studies, Social Sciences, and emerging interdisciplinary fields through research, training, collaboration, and innovation-driven initiatives.

Our Direction

Vision & Mission

Guiding principles that drive our commitment to research excellence, innovation, and societal impact.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized center for research and training that fosters innovation, ethical scholarship, scientific excellence, and sustainable solutions to complex global challenges.

Our Mission

  • To deliver high-quality research training and capacity-building programs aligned with international academic and industry standards.
  • To facilitate cutting-edge, multidisciplinary research that contributes to knowledge generation and evidence-based policy development.
  • To build a collaborative ecosystem connecting researchers, practitioners, industry leaders, policymakers, and institutions worldwide.
  • To uphold the highest standards of ethics, transparency, and academic integrity in all research and training activities.
What We Do

Our Core Activities

SARG Research Biolabs acts as a catalyst for research excellence through diverse initiatives.

Scientific Research

Conducting and facilitating advanced scientific and social research initiatives across multiple disciplines including molecular biology, microbiology, and environmental sciences.

Training & Education

Offering structured training programs, workshops, certification courses, and research mentorship that bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical skills.

Collaborative Research

Supporting translational and collaborative research projects with real-world applications through partnerships with academic institutions and industry.

Innovation Promotion

Promoting innovation through academic recognition platforms, networking opportunities, and knowledge-sharing forums for researchers and scholars.

Policy Advocacy

Advocating evidence-based policy dialogue and socially responsible research practices that contribute to sustainable development.

Strategic Partnerships

Enabling sustainable research development through crowdfunding initiatives and strategic partnerships with national and international organizations.
