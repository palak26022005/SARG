Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about contacting us and our response process.

How long does it take to get a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24-48 business hours. For urgent matters, please call our office directly.

Can I schedule a visit to your laboratory?

Yes! Please fill out the contact form mentioning your interest in visiting, and our team will coordinate a suitable time for your visit.

Do you offer online consultations?

Absolutely! We offer virtual consultations via video call for those who cannot visit in person. Request an online meeting through the contact form.

How can my institution partner with SARG?

Select "Institutional Partnership" in the inquiry type and provide details about your institution. Our partnerships team will reach out to discuss opportunities.

I'm a student. How can I get involved?

Students can apply for membership, enroll in training programs, or participate in workshops. Check our Membership and Programs pages for details.

What languages do you support?

Our primary language of communication is English. However, we can accommodate Hindi speakers as well for most inquiries.
